Who am I?

Well, I am a 23 year old guy from Helsingborg, Sweden - (although I was born in Västerås). At heart, I am a drummer. I've played drums all my life, and that's really my one true passion. Last year I started studying in Malmö, Sweden - at "Teknikhögskolan" to become a web developer. About a month ago I quit there to attend this distance learning course - provided by Lernia and the good fellas from Zocom. I quit "Teknikhögskolan" because within 2 terms we had lost 120+ hours in lesson time. All because the the ministrator didn't plan enough lessons in the education plan, which had a studying tempo of 100%.

My bands at the moment are: Mörbultad - hardcore band from Helsingborg. We are very active all over Sweden and have released 3 albums so far, with our fourth album coming this fall. Our biggest show being support to Phil Anselmo from Pantera in 2014.

My second band Chronus - progressive metal also from Helsingborg, Sweden. I recently joined this band under tragic circumstances - their drummer died in a traffic accident at 19 years old in the beginning of 2015. Despite the tragedy they never stopped looking forwards, and I got the call to join the band in July of 2015, and we've been playing more than ever since then. Really good guys and we very quickly became best friends. Myself and all members of Chronus have a very close relationship with the band Soilwork, from Helsingborg. Chronus guitarist "Svante Furevi" recently played 2 festival gigs with them (one in Essen, Germany - and one in Geneva, Switzerland), as a stand in for their guitarist "David Andersson" who couldn't attend the shows. Soilwork recently mentioned us in a live stream they had, promoting their 10th studio album: THE RIDE MAJESTIC.

What could I teach out to someone else?

I have been a drum teacher for 3 years, teaching children from 6 years up to elders at age 60+. It's never too late to start with and instrument (or basically anything), heart really goes a long way if you want to learn something. A fun exemple is a lady age 43 told me she hadn't pursued drumming because she was afraid of "sheet music"/"drum notes". I immediately wrote down some drum notes and showed them to her. At first she didn't understand a single thing, but I told her that "Within five minutes you can play anything that I write down". She did not believe me at all, so I just gave her my drum notes. I casually explained what they meant, and just like that, she played what I had written down just 2 minutes after being shown it for the first time in her life.
